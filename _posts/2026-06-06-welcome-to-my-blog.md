# Welcome to My Blog!

Well, here we are.

Wait, where is here, and who am I talking about when I say "we"?

I suppose I should take a step back and explain what brought me to writing this blog. Perhaps by explaining why I am leaving my words here for you to read, the question will answer itself.

While I suppose that I require no justification to put my thoughts into writing, it could probably go without saying that I print my considerations so that I might find validation in my opinions or beliefs. After all, everybody wants to feel like they're doing something right every now and then, and I am no exception.

Even more than that, I appreciate building relationships with dialogue, and this is only one of several avenues that I have considered going down in the effort of connecting with individuals, such as yourself.

Speaking of avenues, most of them are two-way streets, and I did say "we". So feel free to reach out - I would love to hear your thoughts just as much as my own. My contact information will be included on every page, and I am even available for conversations - just tap that "Booking" button down below!
